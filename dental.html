Section 00 — Product Overview
DentalOS:
The Market Opportunity

Private dentistry in the UK and US is a £50B+ market dominated by independent owner-operators running fragmented, inefficient businesses with no AI infrastructure. The opportunity to own the intelligence layer of this industry is wide open right now.

The Core Problem We're Solving

The average dental practice uses 6–12 disconnected software tools — a PMS (practice management system), a separate CRM, a social media scheduler, Google Ads managed by an agency, an email platform, a payment system, and more. None of them talk to each other. None of them generate actionable intelligence. The practice owner has no single view of their business, no way to track what's actually generating revenue, and no AI doing anything useful. DentalOS replaces all of it with one connected AI operating system.

The 6 Daily Deliverables
  • Competitor Radar — any practice within 5 miles that changed pricing, launched a new patient offer, updated their website, or got a wave of new reviews overnight. Scraped nightly via Apify. Delivered as a ranked list: most significant changes first.
  • Live Ad Intelligence — what Google and Meta ads your local competitors are running right now. Creative description, messaging angle, estimated spend tier, and how long the campaign has been active. Uses Meta Ad Library API and Google Ads Transparency data — publicly available, fully legitimate.
  • AI Marketing Recommendation — one specific, actionable marketing suggestion generated from competitor movements, seasonal demand data, and the practice's own performance. "Your nearest competitor cut their check-up price by £20. Here are three ways to respond without devaluing your brand."
  • Daily Practice Checklist — a 5-item operational checklist tailored to the practice's current performance gaps. If DNA rates are elevated, the top item is "Review today's high-risk appointments and trigger additional reminders." If treatment plan conversion is down, it's "Follow up on the 3 outstanding implant plans from this week."
  • Dental Industry News — Top 5 — scraped overnight from GDC website, BDA news, NHS BSA, Dental Tribune, and key dental publications. Claude reads everything and surfaces only the 5 most relevant items. Plain English summaries. Link to source.
  • One Marketing Idea — AI-generated campaign concept based on the time of year, local events, recent competitor activity, and practice's current patient acquisition gaps. With copy suggestions, channel recommendation, and estimated effort level.

Core Features

  • Personalised multi-channel sequences — AI generates personalised SMS, email, and WhatsApp messages for each patient segment

  • Campaign scheduler — practice can schedule seasonal campaigns (e.g. January new year, back-to-school)
