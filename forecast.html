Why £10B Is Not Fantasy

Salesforce was worth £10B. ServiceNow was worth £10B. Veeva Systems — a vertical SaaS company serving just the pharma industry — is worth £35B today. What we are building is the Veeva equivalent for three industries simultaneously — starting with the data moat that makes us impossible to displace. The £10B number requires international expansion and sustained execution over 8–10 years. But the unit economics, the market sizes, and the defensibility of the model all support it. The question is not whether it's possible. The question is whether we execute.
